The Problem With Roles

  • New widgets and controls are emerging, driven by the flexibility of the web
  • ...but this is happening on desktop, too!
  • Often these new widgets are hybrids: they blend functionality from one widget with another
  • Assistive Technology APIs have no extension mechanism, currently
  • Screen readers, in particular, rely entirely on the name: there's no equivalence of an affordance
  • End result? Design innovation becomes a barrier to user experience: hard to learn new interactions
